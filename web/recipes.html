Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Toss golden garlic and silky olive oil through spaghetti to make this classic Italian dish – simple,flavorful, and perfect for busy days.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  • Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic and red pepper flakes, sauté for 2 minutes.
  • Add drained spaghetti to the pan, toss well.
  • Garnish with parsley and Parmesan. Serve hot.

Avocado Toast

A quick and healthy breakfast loaded with creamy avocado and fresh toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1/2 lemon (juice)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional)

Instructions:

  • Toast the bread until golden brown.
  • Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread the mixture on toast.
  • Top with red pepper flakes and cherry tomatoes.
  • Enjoy immediately.

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Oatmeal Pancakes

Healthy pancakes made with oats and bananas for a delicious breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 banana, mashed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  • Blend oats into flour.
  • Mix banana, milk, egg, cinnamon, and baking powder.
  • Combine with oat flour to form a batter.
  • Cook pancakes on a greased pan for 2-3 minutes per side.
  • Serve with honey or fruits.
